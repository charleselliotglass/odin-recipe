Elliot's Chicken Wings Recipe

Recipe Summary

Take your chicken wings to the next level using this chicken wings recipe. The secret: adding cornstarch will make your wings extra crispy. So good!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb chicken wings
  • 1 tsp cornstarch
  • 1 cup Frank's Hot Sauce

Steps

  1. Dry the chicken wings and add the cornstarch to the chicken wings.
  2. Set air fryer temparature to 400 degrees and place chicken wings inside.
  3. Fry the chicken wings for 30 minutes.
  4. Retreive the chicken wings and toss in sauce.
  5. Serve with ranch or blue cheese.
